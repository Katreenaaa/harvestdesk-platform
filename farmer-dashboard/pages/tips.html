Agricultural Best Practices

Water Management

  • Implement drip irrigation systems to reduce water usage by up to 60%
  • Schedule irrigation during early morning or late evening to minimize evaporation
  • Install soil moisture sensors for precise watering decisions
  • Collect rainwater for supplemental irrigation needs

Crop Rotation

  • Rotate crops annually to break pest and disease cycles
  • Alternate between deep and shallow-rooted crops for soil health
  • Include nitrogen-fixing legumes to naturally enrich soil
  • Follow a 3-4 year rotation plan for optimal results

Seasonal Planning

  • Align planting schedules with seasonal weather patterns
  • Maintain a comprehensive farm calendar for all activities
  • Plan harvesting during optimal weather windows
  • Schedule maintenance during off-peak seasons

Soil Health

  • Test soil pH and nutrients every 6-12 months
  • Add organic compost to improve soil structure and fertility
  • Practice minimal tillage to preserve beneficial microorganisms
  • Use cover crops to prevent erosion and add nutrients
